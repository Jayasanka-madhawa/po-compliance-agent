# Vendor Onboarding and Status Policy

## Approved vendor criteria
- Completed vendor registration form
- Valid business registration and tax documents
- Bank details verified by finance
- Signed standard terms and conditions

## Vendor statuses
- approved — eligible for auto-accept when all other checks pass
- suspended — not eligible; historical POs require manual review
- pending — new vendor; all orders routed to human review

## Alias matching
- PO vendor name may use trade name if listed in approved vendor aliases
- Fuzzy matches below 85% confidence require human review

## Re-evaluation
- Approved vendors reviewed annually
- Vendors with three flagged orders in 12 months moved to suspended pending audit
