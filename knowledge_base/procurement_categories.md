# Procurement Categories and Controls

## MRO and consumables
- Auto-accept eligible up to standard spending limits
- Examples: bearings, fasteners, lubricants, packaging

## Logistics and freight
- Approved logistics vendors may auto-accept up to LKR 1,000,000
- International freight always requires manager review

## Capital equipment
- Always requires manager review above LKR 500,000
- Must include asset tag request or capex code when available

## IT hardware and software
- Software subscriptions over LKR 200,000 per year require IT security review
- Laptops and servers require IT asset registration

## Professional services
- Consulting engagements above LKR 750,000 require director review
- Must reference master services agreement (MSA) when on file

## Restricted categories
- Hazardous materials — environmental health & safety sign-off
- Gifts and entertainment — prohibited without compliance approval
