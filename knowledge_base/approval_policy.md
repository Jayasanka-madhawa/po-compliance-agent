# Spending Authorization Limits (LKR)

## Auto-approve
- Single purchase order up to LKR 1,000,000 from an approved vendor
- Recurring service contracts up to LKR 750,000 per month

## Manager review
- LKR 1,000,001 to LKR 5,000,000
- Capital equipment between LKR 500,000 and LKR 5,000,000 regardless of category

## Director review
- Above LKR 5,000,000
- Any single line item above LKR 2,500,000

## Emergency procurement
- Up to LKR 250,000 with post-facto manager approval within 48 hours
- Must include justification note on the PO or cover email
