# Payment Terms Policy

## Auto-approve
- Net 15, Net 30, Due on Receipt
- Maximum payment terms for auto-approve: 30 days
- Prepayment allowed only for orders under LKR 250,000 from approved vendors

## Requires human review
- Net 45, Net 60, Net 90
- Any terms above 30 days require procurement manager approval
- Cash on delivery (COD) for orders above LKR 500,000
- Milestone or progress billing on any amount

## Prohibited
- Payment terms beyond 90 days without CFO approval
- Payment in foreign currency (see currency policy)

## Mapping
- "Net 30" = 30 days
- "NET 60 DAYS" = 60 days
- "Due on receipt" = 0 days
- "2/10 Net 30" = 30 days (discount terms ignored for routing)
