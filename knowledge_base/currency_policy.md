# Currency and FX Policy

## Standard currency
- All domestic purchase orders must be quoted and invoiced in LKR
- USD or EUR quotes require treasury approval before acceptance

## Auto-accept rules
- LKR-only orders from approved vendors within spending limits

## Human review required
- Any foreign currency amount on the PO
- Dual-currency quotes (e.g. USD with LKR equivalent)
- Exchange rate not stated on foreign-currency documents

## FX conversion
- Use Central Bank of Sri Lanka daily rate when conversion is required
- Rounding tolerance: 0.5% on converted totals
